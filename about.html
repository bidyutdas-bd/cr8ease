Message From The Founder

With a strong passion for Marketing and helping businesses build an online presence, I felt there was a gap in the market when it came to an integrated approach. With a strong background in the IT Industry and providing IT Solutions, we felt there is a lot of synergy between what we do in IT and in Marketing, with a particular emphasis on Social Media.

There is no better case study than our own. We went through a rebranding exercise in 2022 which has transformed not only the brand of the business, but our mentality. That shift in focus on the brand.

With this, we felt building a strong Social Media presence is the start of every business, however small or large. I have tried it myself, build a social media presence whilst running a business and it is simply not possible. It was time to leave it to the experts.
